Privacy Policy

Your data should not feel like bait.

Last updated: 2026-04-05

EnergyPilot AI is designed as a productivity and energy-awareness product. We aim to collect only the information needed to deliver the service, improve the experience, and understand product demand responsibly.

What we collect

  • Information you submit directly, such as your email address, product intent, and optional form responses.
  • Basic usage signals, such as page visits, button clicks, and planner interactions, if analytics tools are connected later.
  • Local browser storage used for demo or prototype flows.

How we use information

  • To provide the product experience and respond to early access requests.
  • To learn which features, pricing, and positioning resonate with users.
  • To improve product quality, onboarding, and communication.

What we do not do

  • We do not position this service as a medical diagnosis or treatment tool.
  • We do not intentionally sell personal information to third parties.
  • We do not ask for more sensitive data than the product truly needs.

Third-party tools

When connected, EnergyPilot AI may use services such as email providers, payment processors, analytics platforms, or hosting services. Those providers may process data according to their own privacy terms.

Your choices

You can choose not to submit your email, clear locally stored browser data, and contact us to request deletion of information you previously submitted through a live form.
